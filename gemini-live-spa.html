Google I/O 2025: Gemini Live

Conversational AI with Visual Understanding

Gemini Live, Google's advanced conversational AI assistant with powerful vision capabilities, took center stage at I/O 2025. A significant announcement is its broad availability, now free for all Android and iPhone users, making sophisticated AI interaction more accessible than ever.

This feature allows users to engage in natural language conversations with Gemini and, crucially, to share their phone's camera view or screen to get contextual information and assistance based on what the AI "sees" or what's displayed.
