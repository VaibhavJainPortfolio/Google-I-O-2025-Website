Google I/O 2025: Gemini Updates

Advancements in the Gemini Family of Models

Google I/O 2025 brought significant upgrades to the core Gemini models, specifically Gemini 2.5 Pro and Gemini 2.5 Flash. These enhancements are designed to make the models more powerful, efficient, and versatile across a wide range of applications.

The improvements span reasoning capabilities, multimodality (understanding and generating content across different formats), code generation, and long-context understanding, solidifying Gemini's position as a leading AI model.
